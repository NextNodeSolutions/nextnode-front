# Billing Setup

This guide covers everything you need to know about setting up billing for your NextNode account.

## Adding a Payment Method

### Credit Card

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter your card details
4. Click **Save Card**

Your card information is securely stored with Stripe. NextNode never stores raw card numbers.

### PayPal

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Select **PayPal**
4. Log in to your PayPal account to authorize

## Choosing a Plan

### Upgrading to Pro

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Plans**
2. Select **Pro** or **Enterprise**
3. Choose monthly or annual billing
4. Confirm your subscription

**Annual savings**: Get 2 months free with annual billing!

## Billing Information

### Company Details

For business accounts:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Billing Info**
2. Add your company name
3. Add your billing address
4. Add your VAT/Tax ID (if applicable)

### Invoice Recipients

Add multiple email addresses to receive invoices:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Invoice Recipients**
2. Click **Add Email**
3. Enter the email address

## Usage Monitoring

### Tracking Your Usage

Monitor your usage in real-time:

```
Settings → Usage → Current Period
```

You can see:

- Build minutes used
- Bandwidth consumed
- Number of deployments
- Storage used

### Usage Alerts

Set up alerts before hitting limits:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Alerts**
2. Set threshold (e.g., 80% of plan)
3. Choose notification method (email/Slack)

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed

If your payment fails:

1. Check card expiration date
2. Verify billing address matches card
3. Ensure sufficient funds
4. Try a different payment method

### Cannot Upgrade

If you cannot upgrade:

- Verify your email address
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if issues persist
