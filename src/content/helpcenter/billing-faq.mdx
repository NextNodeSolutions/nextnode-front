# Billing FAQ

Find answers to common billing questions below.

## Pricing Plans

### What plans does NextNode offer?

NextNode offers three tiers:

| Plan           | Price     | Deployments | Bandwidth | Team Members |
| -------------- | --------- | ----------- | --------- | ------------ |
| **Hobby**      | Free      | 100/month   | 100 GB    | 1            |
| **Pro**        | $20/month | Unlimited   | 1 TB      | 5            |
| **Enterprise** | Custom    | Unlimited   | Unlimited | Unlimited    |

### How does the free tier work?

The Hobby plan is free forever with generous limits suitable for personal projects and small sites.

### Can I switch plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately:

- **Upgrading**: Prorated billing for the remainder of the period
- **Downgrading**: Credit applied to future invoices

## Payment

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (Enterprise only)
- ACH Direct Debit (US only)

### When am I charged?

- **Monthly plans**: On the same date each month
- **Annual plans**: Once per year on your subscription date

### Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. All pricing is transparent:

- No setup fees
- No cancellation fees
- No bandwidth overage charges (we contact you first)

## Invoices

### How do I access my invoices?

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Invoice History**
3. Download PDF invoices for any period

### Can I add a VAT number?

Yes. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Tax Information** to add your VAT ID.

## Cancellation

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation

Your projects remain accessible until the end of your billing period.

### Will I lose my data?

After cancellation:

- **Hobby tier**: Projects remain on the free tier
- **Data export**: Available for 30 days after cancellation

## Enterprise

### How does enterprise billing work?

Enterprise customers receive:

- Custom pricing based on usage
- Dedicated account manager
- Flexible payment terms (net-30, net-60)
- Custom SLA agreements

Contact [sales@nextnode.dev](mailto:sales@nextnode.dev) for enterprise pricing.
