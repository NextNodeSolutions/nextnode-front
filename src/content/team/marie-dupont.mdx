## About Marie

Marie Dupont brings a unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen to NextNode. Her journey from software engineer to C-suite executive has given her deep insights into what developers need to build exceptional products.

### Vision

Marie founded NextNode with a simple mission: make edge computing accessible to every developer. She believes that the future of web applications lies in distributed infrastructure, and she's committed to building the tools that will get us there.

### Leadership Philosophy

> "Great products come from great teams. My job is to create an environment where talented people can do their best work."

Marie is known for her hands-on leadership style and her commitment to maintaining NextNode's engineering-first culture as the company scales.

### Speaking & Thought Leadership

Marie is a frequent speaker at major tech conferences including:

- **KubeCon** - Keynote on the future of serverless
- **Web Summit** - Panel on women in tech leadership
- **Node Congress** - Talk on building developer-first products

She also writes regularly about startup leadership and engineering culture on the NextNode blog.
