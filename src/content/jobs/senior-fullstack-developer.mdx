## About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Full-Stack Developer to join our growing engineering team. You'll work on building and maintaining our cloud infrastructure platform, collaborating with a talented team of engineers to deliver exceptional developer experiences.

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement new features across our web application stack
- Write clean, maintainable TypeScript code for both frontend and backend
- Collaborate with product and design teams to deliver user-centric solutions
- Mentor junior developers and participate in code reviews
- Contribute to architectural decisions and technical strategy

## Requirements

### Must Have

- 5+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript and modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Next.js, or Astro)
- Experience with Node.js and server-side development
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Cloudflare)
- Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices

### Nice to Have

- Experience with edge computing and serverless architectures
- Knowledge of infrastructure-as-code tools (Terraform, Pulumi)
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience with real-time systems and WebSockets

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary with equity options
- Fully remote work with flexible hours
- Annual learning and development budget
- Modern equipment and home office setup allowance
- Regular team retreats and async-first culture
